Florence Nightingale

“The Lady With the Lamp”

Life of Florence Nightingale

  • Born May 12, 1820 in Tuscany, Italy to a wealthy British family.
  • 1844 - Enters field of nursing against the strong objections of her family
  • October 21, 1854 - Leaves for the Ottoman Empire with a team of nurses to help care for soldiers injured during the Crimean War. Where she reduced death rates from 42% to 2%.
  • August 13, 1910 - Died at home in her sleep
